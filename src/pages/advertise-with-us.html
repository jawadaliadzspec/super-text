The Unique Adspace

Our ad space is embedded in users messages which ensures optimal ad visibility as people are naturally inclined to always want to read their messages.

  • Messages in a group can serve as a carrier fora short message displayed as an "ad footer" at the end of the message that the users receive from their group members.
  • The advertising message is placed in a positive context as part of a desired message.
  • it can be designed for increased contextual relevance based on the interest of the group
  • Opening frequency >97% (almost all messages are read)
  • High click through rates - up to 10%

Large Indonesian community

One of Indonesia's largest communities sought to enhance member engagement by providing a comprehensive app tailored to their needs. Supertext created an innovative application that includes a digital membership card, a Mecca compass for prayer direction, and integrated functionalities for calculating and paying Zakat. To further foster a sense of community, the app offers a chat feature, enabling members to connect and communicate with each other, thereby strengthening their bonds within the community.

Empowering Indonesian Farmers

In the diverse archipelago of Indonesia, farmers are often isolated by geography, making knowledge sharing and collaboration challenging. Supertext bridges this gap by providing an affordable and versatile communication platform accessible via any mobile phone, whether it's a simple feature phone or a modern smartphone. Supertext has proven to be more than just a communication tool; it's a pivotal part of daily farming operations in Indonesia, empowering farmers with the knowledge and connections needed to thrive in a competitive agricultural sector.

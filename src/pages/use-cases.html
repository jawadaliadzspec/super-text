Community Networks

Supertext has collaborated with a wide range of communities over the years, both locally and internationally. Alongside partnerships with the Indonesian scout organization, Nahdlatul Ulama, and farmers in Purworejo, we are also working with sports associations in Sweden, such as Laget.se and Sportadmin.

Whether big or small, all these communities face the common challenge of disseminating information to their members efficiently. Our hybrid solution enables communities to communicate effectively, ensuring messages reach every member, even those offline. Let us know what your needs are! We are here to help and eager to extend our support

Large Indonesian community

One of Indonesia's largest communities sought to enhance member engagement by providing a comprehensive app tailored to their needs. Supertext created an innovative application that includes a digital membership card, a Mecca compass for prayer direction, and integrated functionalities for calculating and paying Zakat.

To further foster a sense of community, the app offers a chat feature, enabling members to connect and communicate with each other, thereby strengthening their bonds within the community.

Empowering
Indonesian Farmers

In the diverse archipelago of Indonesia, farmers are often isolated by geography, making knowledge sharing and collaboration challenging. Supertext bridges this gap by providing an affordable and versatile communication platform accessible via any mobile phone, whether it's a simple feature phone or a modern smartphone.
Supertext has proven to be more than just a communication tool; it's a pivotal part of daily farming operations in Indonesia, empowering farmers with the knowledge and connections needed to thrive in a competitive agricultural sector.

Youth Engagement

Alice, a young student, found herself excluded from her peers’ digital interactions due to her parent’s concerns over certain communication apps. With Supertext, she discovered a safe platform that her parents approved of, enabling her to join friends' chat groups and feel included once again. This scenario underscores Supertext's appeal as a versatile and secure communication solution that caters to diverse user needs.

Sports Associations

How do you ensure that all team members are up to date with the latest news with regards to games, trainings, etc while at the same time not having to worry about what chat tools they all have? One of the larger sports associations in Sweden approached Supertext to hear if by connecting to our API they could give their members a communication tool. Today we have been working together for over 10 years, contributing to a smoother communication between coaches and players - as well as their parents!
